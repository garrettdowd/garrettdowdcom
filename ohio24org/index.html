Ohio 24

The premier ultra-cycling event in the Midwest.

300+ miles. 3 major cities.

Under 24 hours.

The most important competition is the one that you fight within yourself. This event is about pushing your own limits and helping others to do the same.

Ohio provides the perfect environment for novice and experienced ultra-cyclists alike to push their limits. Relatively flat and temperate, Ohio can be traversed from Cincinatti to Cleveland almost entirely on bike trail via the Ohio to Erie Trail.

The inagural Ohio24 event is being planned for late Spring 2022. Taking cues from randonneuring, this event will provide just enough support to make a sub-24 hour time feasible. The route will be at least 500 kilometers and pass through Cincinatti, Columbus, and Cleveland. Participants must apply. Volunteers are welcome.
