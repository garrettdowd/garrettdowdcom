Ohio 24

The premier ultra-cycling challenge in the Midwest.

300+ miles. 3 major cities.

Under 24 hours.

Ohio provides the perfect environment for novice and experienced ultra-cyclists alike to push their limits. Relatively flat and temperate, Ohio can be traversed from Cincinatti to Cleveland almost entirely (90%) on bike trail via the Ohio to Erie Trail (OTET).

Ohio24 is a point-to-point cycling challenge that can be tackled solo or with friends at any time. The route is a minimum of 500 kilometers and traverses Ohio through Cincinatti, Columbus, and Cleveland using most of the OTET. The challenge is to complete the route in less than 24 hours. This challenge can also be attempted as part of an organized annual summer solstice event.
