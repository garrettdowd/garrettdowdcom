Ohio 24

The premier ultra-cycling challenge in the Midwest.

300+ miles. 3 major cities.

Under 24 hours.

Ohio provides the perfect environment for novice and experienced ultra-distance cyclists alike to push their limits. Relatively flat and temperate, Ohio can be traversed from Cincinnati to Cleveland almost entirely (90%) on bike trail via the Ohio to Erie Trail (OTET).

Ohio24 is a point-to-point cycling challenge to traverse Ohio in less than 24 hours, mostly using the OTET. The official route is 500+ kilometers and passes through Cincinnati, Columbus, Akron, and Cleveland. The challenge can be attempted solo or with friends at any time. Don't want to ride 500+ kilometers alone? Think about joining the 2023 solstice event!
