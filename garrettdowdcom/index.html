Garrett Dowd

Technical Leader

Awarded Entrepreneur

Fulbright Scholar

Garrett Dowd is a technical leader with core expertise in mobile robotics and related technologies. With an entrepreneurial background, he has built successful teams to tackle difficult problems and create validated, productize-able solutions. He is a Fulbright, NSF, and UCL alumnus.

Garrett is currently a Technical Lead at the largest applied science research non-profit in the world, Battelle. There he leads new product development team working at the intersection of mobile robotics and micro-location. Prior to Battelle he developed technical strategy for Hyperloop Transportation Technologies.

Garrett holds a masters in business from University College London and a masters in engineering from The Ohio State University. He serves as a Fulbright Alumni Ambassador.
