Garrett Dowd

Autonomy Product Manager

Awarded Entrepreneur

Fulbright Scholar

Garrett Dowd is an internationally experienced product manager with a background in robotics and over six years of experience in autonomous vehicles and transport. He has built and led small teams to build big visions and worked with executive leadership of unicorn startups.

He graduated from University College London with a MSc. Technology Entrepreneurship (MBA) as a Fulbright Scholar, from The Ohio State University with a M.S. in Mechanical Engineering and from The University of Akron with a B.S. in Mechanical Engineering. He is a Fulbright Alumni Ambassador, Citizen Diplomacy Action Fund grant recipient, and a former member of The University of Akron Board of Trustees.

Garrett has lived and worked across five continents. He developed personal electric vehicle designs for Power Plaza in Seoul, worked on autonomous vehicle control algorithms for Tata Consultancy Services in Bangalore, commercialized electric motor technology through National Science Foundation ICorps in San Fransisco, created autonomous vehicle federal policy recommendations in Washington D.C., built digital technology startups in Cleveland, led a State Department funded automation project in Namibia, and developed strategy for HyperloopTT in London, LA, Brazil, and Dubai.

Never without a project, he enjoys dabbling in home automation, hydroponics, hiking, and way too many other things.
